FAQs

Our process involves lying comfortably while a specialized device attaches sensors to your scalp. These sensors connect to a control console that guides you seamlessly into the dream world.

You can explore diverse dreamscapes, from traveling the world and meeting specific people to immersing yourself in fully virtual environments. The possibilities are endless, as long as they comply with our Dream Guidelines. For details on the available dream tiers, please visit our Tiers page. Dreams involving harm to yourself or others, as well as any illegal activities, are strictly prohibited.

Due to regulatory requirements, our service is limited to once per month per individual, and a valid ID is required at the time of purchase. This policy is in place to ensure a balanced and healthy engagement with our dream technology.

Unfortunately, we currently do not offer refunds. We encourage you to carefully consider your purchase to ensure it meets your expectations.

Our service is not recommended for individuals with heart conditions, mental health disorders, psychiatric illnesses, or those under the age of 18. Minors or individuals with these conditions may only use our service if accompanied by an adult guardian who provides informed consent by signing a risk disclosure agreement.
